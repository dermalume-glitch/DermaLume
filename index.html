Analysis Guidelines & Tips

  • Good Lighting: use natural, even lighting. Avoid shadows or harsh flash.
  • In-focus & Close-up: keep the spot sharply in focus and fill most of the frame.
  • Single Spot per Image: use one image per suspicious spot.
  • Clean Background: avoid busy backgrounds or surrounding skin context.
  • Reminder: DermaLume provides preliminary analysis and is not a substitution for professional medical advice.
